




Defensive Abbreviations Explained

TDT - Total Defensive Tackles
DST - Defense Solo Tackles
DAT - Defense Assisted Tackles
DS - Defense Sacks
DSY - Defense Sack Yards
DPD - Defense Pass Defended
DI - Defense Interceptions
DIRT - Defense Interceptions Returned Fumbles
DFF - Defense Force Fumbles
DFR - Defense Fumble Recoveries
FRT - Fumbles Recovered Touchdowns
FRLO - Fumbles Recovered Lost By Opposition
